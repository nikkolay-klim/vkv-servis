About our metalsmith

Vkv-servis offers products made of iron and artistic casting since 1992. Our works are decorated with hundreds of living and office premises. We manufacture both mass and unique products. We manufacture more than a dozen kinds of streetlights, exterior and interior lighting

The company is engaged in the casting business, we can carry protective coating of products up to 6 meters by powder painting, hot metallization of products with zinc
